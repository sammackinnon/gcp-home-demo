Community Governance Platform [DEMO]

This digital platform aims to support community-level governance developmental planning on commonly managed lands.

It will allow community organisations and individuals to access and share data relevant to their community lands, providing better situational awareness and enabling predictive, democratic planning using maps and spatial data. Its applications range from economic to political, cultural and demographic planning. It will also aim to offer training materials for community members to use spatial data and tools.
